template: # ItemTracker - Quick Start Guide (5 Minutes)

Welcome to **ItemTracker** - a professional inventory management system! This quick guide gets you running in minutes.

---

## 🚀 Step 1: Open the App (30 seconds)

1. Open `http://localhost:8000` in your browser
2. You'll see the ItemTracker main screen
3. **That's it!** Now add your first item.

---

## 📦 Step 2: Customize Field Names (1 minute)

ItemTracker is **fully customizable** - make it match your business!

1. Click the **🛡️ Customize Fields** button in the header (top right)
2. You'll see fields like:
   - `Party` → Change to: Client, Store, Supplier, Brand
   - `Month` → Change to: Month, Week, Batch, Season
   - `Size` → Change to: Category, Color, Product, Type
   - `Pieces` → Change to: Units, Items, Boxes, Kilos
   - `Grams` → Change to: KG, Pounds, Liters, Hours

3. **Example for Textile Business** (keep defaults):
   - Party = "Party" ✅
   - Month = "Month" ✅
   - Size = "Size" ✅
   - Pieces = "Pieces" ✅
   - Grams = "Grams" ✅

4. **Example for Retail Store**:
   - Party = "Store"
   - Month = "Month"
   - Size = "Product"
   - Pieces = "Units"
   - Grams = "KG"

5. Click **Save** and the app updates fully!

---

## ✅ Step 3: Add Your First Party (1 minute)

1. Click the **+ button** in the main screen
2. Enter a name:
   - Textile: "Party Name" (e.g., "Shubham", "Fancy Textiles")
   - Retail: "Store Name" (e.g., "Downtown Store")
   - General: Any grouping name
3. Click **Save**
4. See it appear in the list! 🎉

---

## 📅 Step 4: Add a Month (1 minute)

1. **Click on your party** to open it
2. Click the **+ button** under it
3. Enter a month/period name:
   - Month: "January", "February", etc.
   - Or custom: "Week 1", "Spring Season", "Batch A"
4. Click **Save**

---

## 🎨 Step 5: Add a Size/Category (1 minute)

1. **Click on your month** to open it
2. Click the **+ button**
3. Enter size/category name:
   - Textiles: "Small", "Medium", "Large", "XL"
   - Retail: "T-Shirt", "Jeans", "Shoes"
   - General: Any category
4. Click **Save**

---

## 📊 Step 6: Record an Entry (1 minute)

1. **Click on your size** to open it
2. Click **+ entry** button
3. Enter two numbers:
   - **Pieces**: Quantity (how many items)
   - **Grams**: Weight or second metric
4. Click **Save**
5. See totals update automatically! 📈

---

## 🎯 Step 7: Explore Features (1 minute)

### View Analytics
- Click **📊 Analytics** button
- See total summary and statistics
- Understand your inventory at a glance

### View Charts
- Click **📈 Charts** button
- See visual inventory by party
- See cumulative trends over time

### Export Data
- Click **⬇️ Export** button
- Choose format: Excel or PDF
- Share reports with clients/team
- Keep backups

---

## 💾 Step 8: Backup Your Data (1 minute)

Never lose your data!

1. Click **⚙️ Settings** (gear icon)
2. Click **Backup Data**
3. A JSON file downloads
4. Save it somewhere safe (email, cloud, USB)

**To Restore:**
1. Click **⚙️ Settings**
2. Click **Restore Data**
3. Select your backup file
4. Done! All data restored.
